Hyderabad, India

Hyderabad is the financial, economic and political capital of the state Telangana and is one of the most techno savvy cities in India. Hyderabad is known for its opulent heritage in monuments, temples, bazars, masjids, amusement parks, cuisine, gardens etc which reflects the historic legacy of the city. The culture of Hyderabad is a fusion of mannerisms of the people who have come here from across India. This justifies the diversities in language, lifestyle and tradition. The city of Hyderabad is the start-up hub and incubates more than 3000 start-up ideas. Hyderabad was awarded the title the 'software training capital of India. The city accommodates number of multinationals and tertiary sector services, making the city a tough one to beat, in terms of IT advancement.

The only city in India, which was recognized by UNESCO as “the gastronomic city of India” is Hyderabad. From piping hot, melt in the mouth kebabs to ghee dripping Idly’s; from Spicy Hyderabadi Dum Biryani to Sushi; from Irani Chai, Osmania Biscuits to Italian cuisine; from super yummy street food to super luxury 5 star experiences, this city has it all.
